Southern Cassowary Page Layout

My contract job asked me to work on an Southern Cassowary Page Layout for them. They wanted to promote their products on social media by showcasing a map of where their booth will be at the San Diego Comic Con. For this project, I worked on sketching my ideas first then using the Adobe illustrator to help me add sone texture and color to it. As a result, I created about 3 different versions for my supervisor to choose from. It was a fun and rewarding project to do.
