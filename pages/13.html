Coachella Poster

This project was to illustrate an event poster. I chose Coachella because of the style and vibe that it contains. I wanted to design something that is beyond fantasy and space so I picked dark colors that can depict that style. I started off with rough sketches and ended with with two concepts. I enjoy using the pen tool for this process and being able to play more with layer masking in Illustrator.
