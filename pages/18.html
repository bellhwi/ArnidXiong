The Zine Project

The Squid from the Sea is a Zine project that I created in my digital illustration class. We were tasked to illustrate a story of our own. For this piece, I wanted to re-create a storyline similar to Little Mermaid with a little twist. The characters I picked are my two favorite characters, Bun and Squid. I was able to deliver a cute, a fun, and magical story.
