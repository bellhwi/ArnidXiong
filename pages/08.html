Heavenly’s Yogurt Mobile App

As a part of my final project, I was tasked to re-design a website of my choice. I chose Heavenly’s Yogurt because of it’s poor user interface. I believe that I can re-create a better usability for them. To keep things consistent, I kept the theme fun, bright and clean. I used bold yellow color as the primary color, worked on sketching, creating wireframes before using Adobe XD to develop the visuals and prototype.
