Early Letterform

In this assignment, I get to explore early letterforms, the history and symbols. I was tasked to pick a symbol to work with and I chose Sigma. During the design process, I was fascinated to learn about the background history of the symbol. I thought it would be nice to create a form for it and use vibrant colors. The experience of creating this design was enjoyable and fun to do.
